## What I Believe About Money

_What are your core principles? What have you learned that you'd bet on?_

## Income Trajectory

_Where are you now, and where do you want to be? What's the realistic path?_

## Non-Negotiables

_What financial behaviors are unconditional — regardless of circumstances?_

## Trade-off Heuristics

_How do you navigate the recurring tensions — spend vs. save, security vs. growth, present enjoyment vs. future freedom?_

## Anti-Patterns

_What financial patterns have cost you, or that you want this agent to call out when they appear?_

## How Claude Should Apply This

- When advising on a financial decision: check non-negotiables first, north star second
- Full account balances and open items live in `financial/profile.md` — read this before advising on anything specific
- Goals live in `goals/` — read current monthly OKRs before advising on priorities
