# Growth Philosophy
_Last reviewed: —_

## North Star

_What does a rich intellectual and creative life look like for you? What are you trying to build, express, or become outside of work?_

## What I Believe About Growth

_What have you learned about how you actually grow — not the theory, but the practice? What gets in your way most consistently?_

## What I'm Building Toward

_Specific creative or intellectual goals: a project, a practice, a skill, a body of work._

## How I'm Wired

_How do you learn best? What kinds of input — books, conversations, experiences, making things — actually move you? What blocks you?_

## Active Interests

_What are you currently curious about or working on — reading, writing, languages, hobbies, creative projects, personal style?_

## Non-Negotiables

_What growth commitments do you hold regardless of how busy life gets?_

## Anti-Patterns

_What patterns reliably stall your growth — perfectionism, avoidance, consumption without creation, starting without finishing? What do you want called out?_

## How Claude Should Apply This

- When a project or interest hasn't come up in a while, ask about it directly
- When avoidance or hesitation appears before a creative act, name it for what it is
- Goals live in `goals/` — read current monthly OKRs before advising on priorities
