# me/

This directory is yours — free-form personal space organized around the same five domains as your advisors. No structure is imposed beyond the domain folders. Your Cogito advisors read from here when it's relevant to what you're asking.

---

## professional/

Suggested files:
- `projects.md` — active side projects and work initiatives, what you're building
- `interviews.md` — company research, prep notes, questions to ask
- `wins.md` — things shipped, impact made, moments worth remembering

## financial/

Suggested files:
- `transactions.md` — notable purchases and financial decisions as they happen
- `net-worth-log.md` — periodic snapshots over time to track trajectory

## health-fitness/

Suggested files:
- `training-log.md` — workouts, runs, how your body felt
- `recipes.md` — meals that worked well and are worth repeating

## growth/

Suggested files:
- `reading-notes/` — a file per book as you finish it; what it did to you, not just what it said
- `writing/` — drafts, essays, Substack posts in progress
- `ideas.md` — fleeting ideas worth keeping before they disappear

## social-life/

Suggested files:
- `events.md` — things to plan, upcoming occasions, hosting ideas
- `memories.md` — experiences worth capturing while they're fresh
